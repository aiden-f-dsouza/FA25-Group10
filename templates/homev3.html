Everything you need to share and find notes.

Simple tools designed to help UIUC students collaborate effectively.

Post & Edit Notes

Create notes with titles, authors, and course tags. Edit or delete your posts anytime to keep information current and accurate.

Smart Filtering

Filter notes by class (CS124, MATH221, etc.), author, or date range. Sort by recency, title, or author name for easy navigation.

Keyword Search

Search through note titles and content to quickly find exactly what you need for homework or exam prep. Fast and intuitive.

Course Organization

Notes are automatically organized by course. Supports CS, MATH, ENG, RHET, PHY, and more UIUC classes with easy categorization.

Time-Based Views

View notes from today, this week, this month, or all time. Perfect for reviewing recent lectures before exams.

Anonymous Posting

Share notes anonymously if you prefer, or add your name to build a reputation as a helpful contributor in your courses.

Why we built this.

We're a group of UIUC students who got tired of scrolling through endless GroupMe messages trying to find that one set of notes someone shared last week. Class notes shouldn't disappear into chat history. They should be organized, searchable, and accessible to everyone who needs them.

This platform was born from real frustration and built to solve a real problem. We wanted something that felt permanent, trustworthy, and actually useful‚Äînot just another disposable tool.

  • Centralized place for all course notes instead of scattered chats
  • Built with Flask and designed specifically for UIUC courses
  • No ads, no paywalls‚Äîjust students helping students learn better
  • Created as a CS124 Honors group project (FA25-Group10)
